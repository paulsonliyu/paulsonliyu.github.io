Bob's Pizzeria Family

]

Established in 1525 by Bob Pozzuoli, who is originally from Naples, Italy, the birthplace of pizza, Bob's Pizzeria has been passed down from generations to generations for almost 500 years. Throughout the years Bob's Pizzeria has become just as popular with visitors and tourists as it is with native Italian who know where to come for an authentic Italian street slice.
